# maquinitas

## notes on critter and guitari septavox

all the midi notes and messages are sent and received on channel 1.

the controls on the instruments are:

* tone button
* knob 1
* knob 2
* tuning knob
* volume knob
* mode button

options:

| light        | Are           | Cool  |
| --------- |-------------| -----|
| red       | centered      |   $12 |
| yellow    | centered      |   $12 |
| green     | centered   |   $12 |
| light-blue| centered   |   $12 |
| blue      | centered   |   $12 |
| magenta   | centered   |   $12 |
| white     | centered   |   $12 |
